About 10 things you won't be taught in school

Don't let schooling interfere with your Education - Mark Twain Schooling is a good foundation for success but there are experinces, skillsets ect, which are more important and mandatory as the can decide either your success or failure, It happend that our traditional schools doesn't cover those experinces in her curriculum. Therefore let's expand the four walls of our classroom and get new insights.

Met our Impactful speakers

We are going to be learning life long skillsets and Knowledgefrom this program. Therefore we are glad to be having seasoned S.U.G( Student Unoin Government) leaders from various capacities to teach and share their storys, experinces etc, on areas of Education and life worth knowing.

Ogwuru Prince George

Secretary General, SUG (MOUAU)

He is from Abia state in Bende LGA Ozuitem and Igbere and a student of the Michael okpara University of Agriculture umudike currently in 400l. He obtained his NCE from Abia state college of Education Technical Arochukwu in Biology and chemistry before proceeding to MOUAU to obtain his degree in Agriculture specializing in plant pathology.

He is a born-again Christain from the winners chapel and had undergone the Bible School training BCC, LCC and LDC. Prince George runs a foundation known as the Prince George foundation which he uses to reach out to the Girl child for the ladies and Save the next generation for the men. He runs an online program on wealth creation in Agriculture which has imparted hundreds of both graduates and undergraduates. He has served in various capacities in the school as an SRC Hon member, Secretary on Religious matters currently serving as SUG SECRETARY GENERAL and aspiring for SUG president come to the next election. In summary prince, George is an Educationist, Agriculturist, Realtor and lover of Christ and humanity.
